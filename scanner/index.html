Who We Are

Huginn is a security intelligence platform that monitors the internet for exposed services, misconfigurations, and vulnerabilities. We operate a distributed sensor network that performs lightweight, non-intrusive scans to build a database of internet-facing infrastructure.

Our scanning is similar to services like Shodan, Censys, and Shadowserver.

What We Scan

Our scanners perform the following activities:

  • Port discovery — TCP SYN probes to identify open ports (no connection is established)
  • Service identification — Standard HTTP/HTTPS connections to identify web technologies and TLS certificates
  • DNS enumeration — Public DNS record lookups (A, AAAA, MX, TXT, NS, SOA)

We do not perform:

  • Vulnerability exploitation or credential brute-forcing
  • Denial-of-service or high-rate flooding
  • Application-layer attacks or data exfiltration
  • Scanning of opted-out IP ranges

Opt Out

We respect your right to opt out.

If you do not wish your IP addresses or networks to be scanned, send an email to abuse@meethuginn.com with:

  • The IP address(es) or CIDR range(s) you want excluded
  • Your name and organization

We process opt-out requests within 24 hours. Your IP range will be excluded from all future scans.

Legal Basis

Our scanning activities consist of standard network probes (TCP SYN packets, HTTP/TLS connections, DNS queries) that do not access, modify, or exfiltrate data from target systems. These activities are equivalent to connecting to a publicly accessible service with a web browser.

We comply with applicable laws and promptly honour all opt-out requests. If you believe our scanning has caused an issue, please contact us immediately at abuse@meethuginn.com.
